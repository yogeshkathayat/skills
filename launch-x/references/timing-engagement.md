# X Launch Timing + Engagement Runbook

Load at Steps 4 & 8. The golden hour is where an X launch is won — early reply/repost velocity is what
unlocks out-of-network reach.

## When to post

**Default: Tuesday or Wednesday, ~9–10am in your ICP's dominant timezone** (US/EU founder audiences skew ET
morning) — then **trust your own X Analytics ("when your followers are active") over any study.** The two
biggest datasets disagree (Buffer → weekday mid-mornings 9–11am; Sprout → Tue–Thu 12–6pm), which is itself
the proof that **your audience's active window beats the clock.** Avoid weekends and evenings (6–11pm is the
weakest window). Tech/SaaS secondary peaks: ~12–2pm and ~5–7pm ET.

## The golden hour (the single most important thing)

X shows your post to a small follower sample (~5–15%, est.), then expands based on **early engagement
velocity**: 10 replies in the first 15 minutes dramatically out-performs 10 replies over a day, because a
post **loses ~50% of its visibility score every ~6 hours** and a post that doesn't move in its first hour
rarely gets a second chance. (The mechanism is well-supported; specific multipliers — "first 30 min =
1000×", "10 engagements = out-of-network" — are vendor estimates, not from X.) **Front-load genuine
engagement in the first 15–30 minutes.**

## The top lever: be in the thread, replying

In X's open-sourced code, the **single highest positive weight is a reply the author replies back to
(`reply_engaged_by_author = 75.0`)** — vs a plain reply (13.5) and a like (0.5). So **the founder sitting
in the thread, replying to every reply within minutes**, is the highest-leverage launch-day action: each
reply you answer converts a +13.5 into a +75.0 and restarts conversation velocity. Reply fast in the golden
hour, then keep replying all day.

## Asking your audience/team to engage — ALLOWED on X

Unlike Product Hunt / Hacker News, **X has no vote-ring rule** — a genuine "we're live, here's the thread"
ask to your own followers, team, or email list is normal launch behavior. The prohibited line is
**coordinated / reciprocal / compensated** boosting (reply-pods, like-for-like, paid engagement, mass-DM'd
identical scripts) — see `antipatterns-compliance.md`. Compliant asks:

- **Team heads-up:** *"We go live on X at 9am ET tomorrow — thread will be here: [link]. If it's genuinely
  useful, a repost or a real reply helps it travel (only if honest — no scripted comments). I'll be in the
  replies all morning."*
- **Email list:** *"We just launched [Product] 🚀 — we're live on X with the full story: [thread link].
  I'd love your honest take in the replies, and a repost if someone in your network would find it useful."*
- **Pinned invite:** *"We're live. I'll be in this thread all day — bugs, pricing, roadmap, the embarrassing
  parts. Ask me anything and tell me what you'd want it to do next."*

## Quote-posts, pinning, keeping it alive

- **Pin the launch thread** to your profile for the whole campaign (every profile visit is a +12.0 profile
  click that can convert to engagement).
- **Quote-post your own thread at milestones** to re-surface it (each QP is a fresh top-of-feed object).
- Plan a **"content storm," not one tweet**: pre-write 5–10 supporting posts (demo clip, testimonial,
  milestone, FAQ). Thread reach concentrates in the **first ~4 hours** — reply through that window.

## Hour-by-hour first-day runbook (T+0 = main post)

| Time | Action |
|------|--------|
| **T−24h to T−1h** | Pre-write the thread + 5–10 supporting posts; give team + email list a heads-up that you go live tomorrow. |
| **T+0** | Post the launch thread (with native demo media); put the link in the first reply. |
| **T+0–2 min** | **Pin** the thread; fire the team + email "we're live" notes (genuine, individual — not a pod). |
| **T+0–30 min (golden hour)** | Reply to **every** reply within minutes; ask follow-ups to keep each sub-thread going. Don't leave the screen. |
| **T+30–60 min** | Keep replying; post supporting tweet #1 (the demo clip) as a reply/quote-post. |
| **T+1–4h** | Reply to everything (reach peaks in ~4h); quote-post once with a new angle; engage adjacent ICP threads. |
| **Midday (~T+4–6h)** | First **milestone quote-post** ("N signups in M hours — thank you"); repost a notable reply/testimonial. |
| **Afternoon (~T+6–9h)** | Supporting post (FAQ / how-it-works / objection-handler); clear the reply queue. |
| **Evening (~T+9–12h)** | **Recap quote-post**: day-one numbers, best replies, what's next, restated CTA; thank supporters by name. |

## Post-launch (Step 8)

The 72 hours after launch matter most: a daily milestone quote-post, reply to stragglers, and repurpose the
best moments into other channels. Keep the thread pinned while the campaign runs.
