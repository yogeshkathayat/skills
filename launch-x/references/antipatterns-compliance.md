# X Launch Anti-Patterns & Compliance

Load at Steps 5 & 7. Unlike Product Hunt / Hacker News, **X has no vote-ring rule — asking your own
audience to engage is allowed.** The constraints split into three lines: a **rule line** (X policy — can
derank/lock/suspend/demonetize), an **optimization line** (legal but hurts reach), and what's **fine**.

## The one-liner

- **RULE LINE (X policy violations):** engagement bait ("like & RT to win", "reply YES and I'll DM you the
  link", follow-for-follow), buying/trading engagement or followers, engagement pods / reciprocal-engagement
  groups, metric-inflation services, "follow churn", bulk/aggressive auto-follow, mass/automated unsolicited
  replies or DMs, automated likes, bulk auto-reposts, duplicate cross-account posting.
- **OPTIMIZATION LINE (legal but hurts reach — mostly third-party estimates):** external link in the **main
  post** (→ put it in the first reply), 3+ hashtags, delete-and-repost re-rolls, anything that earns
  blocks/mutes/reports.
- **FINE (don't over-correct):** asking **your own** audience to check it out / repost / reply; the link in
  the first reply; DMing people who **opted in**; manual, individual engagement; quote-posting your own thread.

## Rule line (X's written policies — verbatim where quoted)

From the **Authenticity** policy and **Automation Rules** (captured mid-2026):

- Prohibits *"coordinating with and/or compensating others to conduct account metric inflation in any X
  features, including Likes, Polls, Replies, Reposts, Lists, Views, or Follows,"* and using/promoting
  third-party services to do so. (Covers **engagement pods** and bought engagement.)
- **Content Spam** — posting *"in a bulk, duplicative, irrelevant or unsolicited manner"* — is prohibited
  (this catches **mass-identical replies** and **DM blasts**).
- **Automation:** no **unsolicited bulk/automated DMs** (only to users who opted in, with an easy opt-out —
  *following you is not consent*); no keyword-triggered **auto-replies**; **automated likes are banned
  outright**; no **bulk/aggressive auto-follow/unfollow** or bulk **auto-reposts**.
- **Engagement bait** that incentivizes artificial interaction is engagement manipulation and a creator-
  **monetization disqualifier**.

Enforcement scales with history: anti-spam challenge / account lock → removal from Search & For-You →
temporary lock → suspension / demonetization.

## DO / DON'T (the launch checklist)

**✅ DO (safe + reach-positive)**
- Main post is **text-first** (or text + native image/video); **no external link in the main tweet**.
- Put the launch URL in the **first reply** (UTM-tagged).
- Ask your **own** audience plainly: *"Just shipped X — would love your thoughts, and a repost if it's
  useful."* (organic asks are fine).
- Optimize for **conversation**: ask a real question; reply to every commenter fast in the first 60 min
  (replies ≫ likes).
- DM only people who **opted in** (asked / replied / already in a thread); give an easy opt-out.
- 0–2 hashtags. If a post flops, write a **new, better** one — don't delete-and-repost the same words.

**❌ DON'T (rule-violating)**
- "Like & RT to win", "RT for a chance", "reply YES and I'll DM you the link", follow-for-follow.
- Buy followers/likes/reposts/views; join engagement pods; use metric-inflation services; "follow churn".
- Blast the same reply under dozens of posts; send bulk/unsolicited/automated DMs.
- Automate likes; keyword-triggered auto-replies; bulk auto-reposts; duplicate cross-account posting.

## The allowed vs banned ask (the line to teach)

- **Allowed:** *"Just launched [product] after 6 months — here's what it does [thread]. If it's useful, a
  repost would mean a lot, and I'll be in the replies all day."* (genuine audience, no prize, no bots).
- **Banned (engagement bait):** *"Like & RT to win a free [product]!"* / *"Reply YES and I'll DM you the
  link"* / *"Follow + follow back."*

## The banned-phrase / bait scan (run over the post + any sharing message)

Flag (case-insensitive) — any hit = rewrite: `like & rt to win`, `rt to win`, `retweet to win`, `reply
YES`, `reply DONE`, `follow for follow`, `follow back`, `f4f`, `tag 3`, any prize/incentive tied to a
like/RT/follow, and any plan to **auto** or **bulk** DM/reply/follow/like. (Asking for a genuine
repost/reply with no incentive is **not** a violation.)
